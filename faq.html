What is MyTunes?

MyTunes is a music streaming platform designed to provide users with personalized playlists, music recommendations, and an extensive library of genres and artists. Our goal is to enhance your music experience by curating playlists that fit your mood and preferences.

What are the different subscription plans available?

We offer a variety of subscription plans to cater to different needs:

  • Basic: Generate up to 5 personalized playlists per month.
  • Standard: Generate up to 20 personalized playlists with advanced customization.
  • Premium: Unlimited personalized playlists with full customization and additional features.
  • Family: All Premium features for up to 6 family members.
  • Business: Custom solutions for business needs.
  • Enterprise: Advanced features for large organizations.
  • One-Time Purchase: Lifetime access to all features with a one-time payment.
  • Add-Ons: Extra playlist packs with specialized themes or genres.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign in / Register" button on the top-right corner of our website. Follow the prompts to enter your details and set up your account. Once registered, you can start exploring our music library and enjoy personalized playlists.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email. If you need further assistance, contact our support team at support@mytunes.com.

How do I update my account information?

Log in to your account and navigate to the "Account Settings" page. Here you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage your subscription settings.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical problems, please check our Support Page for troubleshooting tips. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team at support@mytunes.com for further assistance.

Is MyTunes available on mobile devices?

Yes, MyTunes is available as a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store to enjoy your music on the go.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account and go to the "Subscription Management" section. Follow the prompts to cancel your subscription. You will continue to have access to your plan until the end of the current billing cycle.
